Dunkirk:

The Evacuation of British Expeditionary Forces and French Troops From the Beaches of Dunkirk, France.

Evacuation

The whole:

The evacuation of Dunkirk, or Operation Dynamo, was the evacuation of British Expeditionary Forces and French troops on the beaches of Dunkirk between the 26th of May, and June 4th.
On May 22nd 1940, the German High Command, with Hitler's approval issued a halt order. The objective of preventing the evacuation was now granted to the Luftwaffe (German Air Force). Details...
