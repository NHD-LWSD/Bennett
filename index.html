The whole:

The evacuation of Dunkirk, or Operation Dynamo, was the evacuation of British Expeditionary Forces and French troops on the beaches of dunkirk between the 26th of May, and June 4th.
On May 22nd 1940, the German High Command - with Hitler's approval- issued a halt order. The objective was to prevent the evacuation and the order was given to the Luftwaffe (German Air Force). Details...
